Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

RunWrapped is designed with privacy as a core principle. All processing happens locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any servers.

Health Data

RunWrapped reads running workout data from Apple Health with your explicit permission. This data is used solely to generate a run recap video on your device. Health data is never uploaded, stored remotely, or shared with third parties.

Photos

If you choose to add a photo to your video, the image is accessed locally and used only for rendering. Photos are never uploaded or shared externally by RunWrapped.

Data Collection

RunWrapped does not collect analytics, tracking data, personal identifiers, or usage metrics. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Third Parties

RunWrapped does not share data with third parties.
